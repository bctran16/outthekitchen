Bang Tran

I am Bang Tran, and I am currently a junior at Boston University. I am an avid homecook that specializes in Vietnamese and Korean food. Some of my favorite dishes are phở, sundubu jigae, bún chả, and kimbap . Out of the kitchen, I love traveling and trying food from all over the world. I have done (self-guided) food tour in Montreal, Quebec City, Boston, New York, and Hanoi .
